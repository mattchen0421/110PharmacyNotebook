## 分類
### Organ-based
- Vulvitis 外陰炎
- Vaginitis 陰道炎
- Cervicitis 子宮頸炎
- Endometritis 子宮內膜炎
- Salpingitis 輸卵管炎
- Oophoritis 卵巢炎
### Course
- Acute, Chronic, Recurrent
### Pathogens
- Virus
- bacteria
- chlamydia
- fungus
- protozoa
## Clinical Features
- Local symptoms
- Systemic symptoms
- Pelvic Inflammatory Disease (PID) 
	- Tubo-ovarian abscess 輸卵管卵巢膿腫 
	- Peritonitis 腹膜炎
## Pelvic Inflammatory Disease (PID) 
- Ascending infection
	- Vulva→vagina→cervix→ endometrium→fallopian tubes→pelvic cavity 
- Pain, vaginal discharge
- 並發症 Complications
	- 不育症 infertility and 子宮外孕 ectopic pregnancy
